Tell us about working
with a Thriving

Lambeth Together has commissioned Solico to understand what Thriving Communities organisations need in order to engage with and deliver for Integrated Neighbourhood Teams. The organisations themselves are one part of that picture. The other part is how it feels to work alongside them, and that is where you come in.

Your name is captured so we can follow up if needed, but it stays with Solico. Everything we share with the Thrivings, Lambeth Together, or anyone else is anonymised. Nobody will know the feedback came from you.
