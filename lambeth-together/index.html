Thriving Communities · Lambeth

Maturity mapping
preparation pack

Lambeth Together has commissioned Solico to understand what Thriving Communities organisations need in order to engage with — and deliver for — Integrated Neighbourhood Teams. We want to hear your views on what would help. This pack is part of that process.

Before we visit, Solico will form an initial view of where your organisation sits. When we meet, we want to compare notes. Your version matters most — this pack gives you the chance to share it.
